Romila Pradhan

I am a Postdoctoral researcher in the Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute at the University of California San Diego. Before joining the University of Califorina San Diego, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate and Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University, where I worked with Prof. Sunil Prabhakar on problems related to the management, integration and mining of uncertain data. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. My dissertation focused on capitalizing user feedback and the knowledge of relationships among claims of data items to improve conflict resolution of data integrated from multiple information providers, and applying the learnings in the context of false data to propose a framework for combating false information on the Internet. Previously, I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India with M.S. and B.S. in Mathematics and Computing.
